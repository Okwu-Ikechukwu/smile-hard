ABOUT LOVERS TRANSPORT

Lovers Transit is a Trusted Name In Transportation for few months and counting. Lovers provides transportation services for intercity/interstate travelers in
Nigeria. Lovers customers can enjoy air conditioned vechicles and light entertainment. Lovers Transit Ltd offers you the unique service to hire a bus that will
take to anywhere in Nigeria, following your desired journey plan, schedule and duration at a very affordable price. Lovers also provides haulage services for
businesses and individuals looking to transport large goods and services or materials within and around Nigerian cities.
